Regular Jeopardy Rules:
 - Most points win
 - Wrong answers loses points
 - Right answers wins points
 - Team who buzzes first gets to answer
 - Teams who buzz must answer within 5 seconds
Special Rules:
 - The first person to get a question wrong will get to pick the next category unless someone after gets the question right
 - Multiple Daily Doubles
 - Teams may spend 50% of the points a question is worth to force an opponent to answer
 - 1 Phone call per team
 - Teams may answer a question multiple times if no other team wants to attempt
